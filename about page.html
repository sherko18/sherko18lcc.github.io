Hi, My name is Stanislav, I am Contemporary Communication student at LCC International University which is based in Klaipeda, Lithuania. I am originally from Lviv, Ukraine. I moved to Europe about 1,5 years ago and since I started to do photography of all kinds. I do not have a specific style I work in; I am just trying to capture beauty in every moment I get to shoot, it might be landscape or portrait, it does not matter for me. If you are interested in my works I would encourage you to follow me on Instagram, and if you want to we can arrange photoshoot.
